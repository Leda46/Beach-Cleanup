Beach Cleanup Game

Save the beach by cleaning up trash and avoiding penalties!

How to Play

Click on trash to collect it and earn points!

Avoid penalties, with red borders as they reduce your score!

Collect power-ups with yellow borders to gain extra points or bonuses.

You have 30 seconds to clean as much as possible!
